History

Happy Whitewater Rafting Company was founded in 1980 and has led countless tourists to explore the magnificent and beautiful Xiuguluan River in Taiwan. We have the latest boats and life-saving equipment, professional tour guides and lifeguards who receive regular training to provide customers with safe and exciting rafting activities.
